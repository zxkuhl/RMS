Plugins

Plugins extend Discord with new features. Enable or disable them individually in RMS Settings → Plugins.

How to Use Plugins

1

Open Discord Settings

Click the gear icon at the bottom left of Discord.

2

Go to RMS → Plugins

Scroll down in settings to find the RMS section, then click Plugins.

3

Toggle plugins on or off

Click the toggle next to any plugin to enable or disable it. Some plugins have a settings gear icon for extra options.

Plugin Settings

Many plugins have their own settings. Click the gear icon next to a plugin name to configure it. Settings are saved automatically.
